About Me

I'm a Ph.D student in materials science, working on knowledge discovery from materials to develop and design novel materials; now I focus on the development of materials characterization techniques that combined with machine learning.

I'm interested in what kind of change will occur in the world by utilizing machine learning and related technologies, and I would like to contribute to realizing a better world from an industrial standpoint.
